Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Formula One technology and racing

What makes Hanu Studio Racing different from other F1 teams?

We combine cutting-edge aerodynamic research with revolutionary hybrid power systems, focusing on championship-winning technology that pushes the boundaries of what's possible in Formula One racing.

How can I join the Hanu Studio Racing team?

We welcome applications from engineers, mechanics, data analysts, and racing professionals. Visit our careers section to explore current opportunities in aerodynamics, hybrid systems, and race operations.

What technology areas do you specialize in?

Our expertise spans aerodynamics, hybrid power systems, advanced materials, data analytics, and race strategy. We also lead development in ERS technology and computational fluid dynamics.

Do you offer internships or graduate programs?

Yes! We offer competitive internship programs for engineering students and graduate development programs. Applications are reviewed quarterly, with mentorship from championship-winning engineers.

How do you ensure safety in high-speed racing?

Safety is our top priority. We exceed all FIA safety standards through advanced crash testing, innovative materials, and continuous collaboration with safety experts to protect our drivers at 300+ km/h.
